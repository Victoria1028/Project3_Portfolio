I am currently attending Illinois State University, where I will graduate with two majors: a B.F.A. in Graphic Design and B.A. in Studio Arts in Sculpture.

My plans for the future are to intern at various locations and eventually end up in a position for exhibit design.

My main interest in design is interactive exhibit design, as well as inclusivity for diverse cultural groups and peoples with various disabilities.

Within Design

When I’m not designing, I enjoy spending time working on non-digital art forms such as painting and collaging.

In my personal life, I volunteer my time for various causes, including meals at my grandparents’ church, a homeless shelter, and a mission in Kentucky that helps those in need receive much-needed work on their homes.
